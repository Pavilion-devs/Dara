The Problem

Every trade you make is public.

Wallet watchers track your moves. Snipers front-run your launches. MEV bots exploit your trades. Your on-chain history tells your entire story.

Wallet Tracking
Copy traders follow profitable wallets. Your alpha is everyone's alpha.
Sniper Bots
Launch a token and bots buy before you can. Your own creation, front-run.
Rug Pulls
No way to know if a token is safe before you swap. Hope isn't a strategy.

The Solution

Break the on-chain link.

Dara uses a server-side relayer to execute your trades. Your wallet deposits SOL, but the swap transaction only shows the relayer. Tokens land in a fresh stealth wallet.

Anonymous Swap
Jupiter swaps via relayer. Your wallet never appears in the swap transaction.
Stealth Launch
Create tokens via Anoncoin + instant pre-buy into multiple stealth wallets.
AI Risk Analysis
GPT analyzes holder concentration, authorities, and flags rug-pull risks before you swap.
